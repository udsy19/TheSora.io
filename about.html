Finding beauty in the spaces between moments

Udaya Vijay Anand

My journey in photography began in 2018 when my uncle took me on a photography trip and generously gave me his camera. That moment sparked a creative passion that has only grown stronger over the years.

I believe that photography is more than just capturing images—it's about preserving moments, telling stories, and creating tangible memories of the fleeting experiences that make up our lives.

My approach focuses on authenticity and connection. Whether I'm photographing a graduation milestone, a creative portrait session, or documenting special events, I strive to capture genuine emotions and create images that resonate with depth and meaning.

Journey

Key milestones and achievements that have shaped my photographic journey from first exposure to professional recognition.

2018
Where It All Began
Started my photography journey when my uncle took me on a photography trip and gave me his camera, which sparked my fascination with visual storytelling.
2019
Building Foundations
Attended my first photography course on the Basics of Photography in Chennai, establishing a strong foundation in technical skills.
2020
Expanding Horizons
Completed certification and 150 hours of flight time to become a certified drone pilot under DJI. Nominated for Best Photographer of the Year at Kodaikanal International School. Completed Intermediate level Photoshop and Lightroom certification.
2021
Mastering the Craft
Earned certification in studio light portraits and another in Composition and Negative Spaces. Nominated for Sony Youth recognition. Served as head photographer for the 2020-2021 yearbook.
2022
Professional Recognition
Had images published in the KIS Alumni Magazine. Continued as head photographer for the 2021-2022 yearbook. Third consecutive nomination for Photographer of the Year at KIS.

Expertise

Areas of specialized knowledge and technical abilities that define my photography style and professional capabilities.

Portrait Photography
Specialized in creating authentic, emotive portraits that capture the unique personality and essence of individuals. Skilled in natural light, studio setups, and environmental portraiture.
Drone Photography
Certified drone pilot with 150+ hours of flight time and expertise in aerial photography, providing unique perspectives and stunning landscapes from above.
Event Photography
Experienced in capturing the key moments, emotions, and details of various events, from intimate gatherings to large celebrations and fashion shows.
Post-Processing
Certified in Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom with intermediate to advanced skills in photo editing, color grading, and image enhancement while maintaining authenticity.
Studio Lighting
Professional training in studio lighting setups, manipulating light to create mood, dimension, and visual interest in controlled environments.
Composition
Advanced understanding of compositional techniques, including the use of negative space, leading lines, and framing to create visually compelling and balanced images.
